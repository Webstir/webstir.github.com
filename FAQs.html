What is Webst.ir?

Webstir is the last bookmark you'll ever need. It is a repository for all the interesting, innovative, and best websites, web apps, and content. At Webstir, we see websites as simply another creative tool & we celebrate & share sites that serve great content in new and interesting ways. Yum.

What stops Webst.ir from being a directory, another bookmark site, or really just a set of links?

Technically, nothing. What makes Webstir unique and relevant is that is one part crowdsourced, one part editor. This way you get all the great content available, but still a gatekeeper to maintain quality. Also, we like that the internet provides new, more efficient ways to educate, conduct business, share, connect, create, and appreciate the world. Our mission is to allow individuals, groups, and even all of humanity to self-actualize their dreams through access to these resources.

How did Webst.ir come to be?

One day, Ben submitted the concept of a meta-web subreddit to reddit. He left for the day, and by the time he came back it had a couple thousand subscribers! It was clear that people were excited about this idea so Webst.ir allows this concept to be so much more.

What does Webst.ir mean anyway?

Webstir is a nice pun because it's stirring up the web and it gives credit to Noah Webster, the co-creator of the dictionary. And like everything webby, you take something that already exists, and you misspell it.

What tools are you using for the site?

We used Panabee to find a name. We're hosting free on Github, using Bootstrap and the free Spacelab theme from Bootswatch. We're using GitHub for Mac, and Sublime Text 2. We learned a lot from W3 schools, Google searching, and Codecademy. We've honed our photoshop skills watching You Suck at Photoshop. We're using disqus for the comments, and WePay for our donate button. It's all open source, not for profit, collaborative, etc. (Hey, it's the web, isn't it?!)

What kind of content can be submitted?

We try not to limit it, but some company's website, a small blog, a news site, AOL- MEH. Are these sites that excite anyone? They key component is great and original content, so that can either be how the site is structured, how it's helping creativity, a useful purpose, or the content or overall reach of the site.

What about blogs?

There are so many damn good blogs out there. We don't want to be spammed with every one of them, however if there is something innovative about the blog, or it happens to have a very large captive viewing audience or just great content, by all means.

Why is xyz website on the list?

If you find a website that you think is garbage, inappropriate, or doesn't deserve to be here, please comment in the suggest section.

You put xyz in the category Q? Wouldn't it make much more sense in P?

One of the constraints of the current way the site is organized is things have to be a single category, and many websites cross over. That said, we try to keep like things together but if we messed up, you think something should be moved, or a new category made, just mention it in the suggest section. New sorting & search methods will come online as the site develops further.

Are there any sponsored websites or paid placement?

No. Whenever you bring in the profit motive, the viewer must be skeptical of what they are seeing. This is simply a list of great websites. If a site seems more advertisement than innovative, let us know and we'll delete it.

Why are you doing this?

We have spent much of our lives on the internet exploring all of its wonder, beauty, laughs, shock, and insight. We would simply like to discover & share the abundance of the web and help others do the same.

This sounds great! Are you looking for help?

Absolutely! Please say what skill set you have, and how you would be interested in contributing. Obviously, web coders, designers, and developers are welcome; Graphic Designers, writers, UI experts, marketing people, and internet fanatics. But maybe you don't want to do any of these things, and simply want to sift through and discover new websites, promote Webst.ir, or have some other great idea! E-mail: help@webstir.org

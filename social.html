Social Connections

The internet from very inception was about connecting people. With every individual now having a personable computer and an internet connection, people can socialize, discuss, chat, flirt, collaborate, and organize all from the comfort of their desk. There have been countless forums, blogs, chatrooms, messaging systems, and various ways to communicate and connect. The websites featured here are some leaders in the field of how we can connect, share, understand one another, and the world.

Open Education

For centuries learning has been restricted to institutions and academia. It often costed thousands of dollars, was very competitive, and open only to a select few. The internet opens up the availability of information and thus has allowed anyone and everyone to have access to the best courses, professors, and lectures. It has also allowed interactive game-based learning that can be more engaging that sitting through a lecture.

Unlocking Creativity

The web has allowed everyone to be an artist, a publisher, and a producer. It has given tools that allow individuals to achieve their vision and share it with the world in a way that is cost effective, and any good idea can go viral. This has allowed everyone to express themselves in any manner, and share that expression with the world.

Kickstarter

Kickstarter is a funding platform for creative projects. Everything from films, games, and music to art, design, and technology. Kickstarter is full of ambitious, innovative, and imaginative projects that are brought to life through the direct support of others. Since the launch in April, 2009, over $350 million has been pledged by more than 2.5 million people, funding more than 30,000 creative projects.

The Game Crafter

The Game Crafter (TGC) is the world’s first web-to-print game publishing company and offers a print on demand game publishing service. TGC empowers game designers around the world by allowing them to make a board game, card game, and custom playing cards through a website. Using the latest web technology, TGC developed a website platform that simplifies the design, manufacturing, and retail processes related to tabletop games.

Half Bakery

The Halfbakery is a communal database of original, fictitious inventions, edited by its users. It was created by people who like to speculate, both as a form of satire and as a form of creative expression. This site is not a store, a game, a business of any sort, or a resource to help people guide their inventions from conception to completion. This is the place where you post the things you're not going to be working on - because you can't be bothered, or you don't know how to, or because it's not such a stellar idea after all.

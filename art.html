Free Art

The web has largely taken away the cost and the restrictions over media. There is an abundance of websites to stream movies, music, and download books. Because digital media is non-physical, there is close to zero cost to each use of the media. Piracy has become widespread and most of humanities famous works can be downloaded for free. Additonally, this low-cost distrubution model has transitioned many companies into solely ad-based models.

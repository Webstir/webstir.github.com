Exchange Without Obstacles

Trade has always been central to who we are and how we work together. It is what has allowed humans to have a continually rising standard of living. The internet has enabled low-cost trade that results in much higher efficiency, greater wealth, and opens up opportunities like peer to peer transactions, asset sharing, as well as allowing huge complex organizations to be managed from the comfort of one's home.
